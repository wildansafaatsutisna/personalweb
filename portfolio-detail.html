KeyBoard Project

Branding

KeyBoard is a premium mechanical keyboard brand. The project involved creating a brand identity that reflects the premium quality and modern aesthetic of the product. The challenge was to create a minimalistic yet distinctive brand that would appeal to both gaming enthusiasts and professionals.

Client

KeyBoard Inc.

Date

June 2023

Services

Brand Identity, Logo Design, Packaging

Live Preview Case Study

Project Gallery

Design Process

01

Research & Discovery

We conducted extensive research on the mechanical keyboard market, target audience, and competitors to identify opportunities and inform our design decisions.

02

Concept Development

Based on our research, we developed several concept directions for the brand identity, focusing on minimalism, premium quality, and technical excellence.

03

Design & Refinement

After selecting a direction, we refined the design elements, including logo, color palette, typography, and visual language to create a cohesive identity.

04

Implementation & Delivery

We created comprehensive brand guidelines and applied the new identity to various touchpoints, including packaging, website, and marketing materials.
